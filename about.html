History

歴史

ERamen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle. Customer can find any kind of ramen, such as ramen and so many kind of tempura.

Vision

ヴィジョン

Our vision is to create a welcoming and authentic ramen experience. We strive to be a go-to destination for ramen lovers, where they can indulge in delicious and soul-satisfying bowls of ramen crafted with passion and attention to detail.

Mission

ミッション

Our mission is to serve exceptional, authentic ramen that delights the senses, nourishes the body, and creates memorable experiences for our customers. We are committed to upholding the traditions of Japanese ramen while embracing innovation and creativity to deliver unique and flavorful dishes.
